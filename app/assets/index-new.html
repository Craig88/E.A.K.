E.A.K. is an online platform game, made by Drum Roll, that teaches kids to code and create on the web. It does this in a unique way, by encouraging kids to hack into levels - written in HTML and CSS - in order to complete the game.

E.A.K. is free and open source, and we need your help to make it great:

If you're a teacher in the UK or know anyone who is, we'd love to visit and observe classes playing our game.
If you're a teacher outside of the UK, it would be great if you could play the game with your students, and tell us what you think!

We're also eager to chat to developers, designers, educators, and anyone else who would like to help us build E.A.K.

Contact us at humans@godrumroll.org, and fill out the details below to keep up to date with all things E.A.K.
