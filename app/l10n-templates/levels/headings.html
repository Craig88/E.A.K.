Ahhh, headings!

The HTML heading are a very big family... They go all the way from <h1> (the most important), to <h6> (the least important).

And between you and me, they argue like a fresh bucket of shrews before a steak-off!

You might have your page title as a <h1>

and a subtitle as a <h2>, for example

These first two headings didn't get you very far into the cave.

If you created more, you might be able to reach the bottom

Add a new <h3> tag at the bottom of the code.

Start with a <h3>. Then add some text. Finish it off with a </h3> - remember the /!

<h3> Soup Party! </h3>

Add some more tags.

You've got a <h3>, now you just need to do the same thing for <h4>, <h5>, and <h6>.
